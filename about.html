About MegansLaw.com

Inspired by the tragic and senseless death of Megan Kanka, MegansLaw.com was created to educate parents about the potential threats sexual predators and registered sex offenders pose to our children. MegansLaw.com is not associated with any government agency or department.

About KidsLiveSafe

Kids Live Safe is an innovative organization focused on protecting kids from Sexual Predators. Since 2009, they have provided top-rated, web-based child protection tools to safeguard our most vulnerable citizens. Kids Live Safe is not a government entity.

What does Kids Live Safe offer?

Kids Live Safe provides the most comprehensive family protection solution on the market to assist parents and caregivers in keeping their children safe from sexual predators.

Actionable Information.

There are over 750,000 registered sex offenders in the U.S. With Kids Live Safe you can do unlimited sex offender searches anywhere in the country. Searches can be conducted by city, zip code or first/last name. Search results include comprehensive offender profiles which include color photo, full name, aliases, home address, description of the crime committed and customizable maps to identify the exact location of the offender.

Critical notifications.

Kids Live Safe provides active family protection zone monitoring of up to four locations. Kids Live Safe will monitor each location and send immediate Email Alerts when a registered sex offender moves into a protection zone.

Rapid Response

Kids Live Safe provides digital Child Emergency Response Profiles which can be set up for each of your children. These digital profiles contain a current picture of your child, and all pertinent personal information to help you recover your child if they are lost or abducted. Each profile also includes medical information that can be used to assist emergency response personnel.

Online Peace of Mind.

Kids Live safe provides one of the highest rated internet filtering tools on the market today – Net Nanny. With Net Nanny you can protect your child while they spend time on the internet. Not only does Net Nanny protect your children from sexual predators but it also protects your children from online pornography, cyberbullying and more.
